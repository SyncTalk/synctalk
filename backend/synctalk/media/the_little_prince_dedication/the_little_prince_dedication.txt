The Little Prince 

Antoine de Saint-Exupery 

To Leon Werth 

Children, I am sorry I have dedicated this book to a grown-up person. But I have a good excuse: this grown-up person is the best friend I have in the whole world. I have another excuse: this grown-up person understands everything, even children’s books. And here’s a third excuse: this grown-up person lives in France, where he’s hungry and cold. He needs something to make him feel better. But if all these excuses aren’t enough, I would like to dedicate the book to the child this grown-up person used to be. All grown-up people started off as children. Though very few of them remember that. So here is my corrected dedication: 

To Leon Werth

When he was a small boy
